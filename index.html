CHALLENGE COMMUNITY SERVICES

Support for our community.

Challenge Community Services has provided innovative and people-focused services to the community for over 60 years. Our offering to regional and metropolitan communities includes Disability Services, Foster Care, Allied Health and Business Services.

Support to reach your full potential

Our therapeutic team can assist you to remove barriers and reach your full potential.

We understand that many people will experience difficulties with relationships, personal wellbeing and emotional health at some point.

Tell Me More

We all have dreams.

Whether it’s to land a good job, live independently or be part of a secure, loving family, we all want to belong.

That’s why Challenge Community Services supports our inclusive community. We’ll help raise you up to meet your goals. We’ll be there to support you when life gets tough.

Take a look around our site and see for yourself the multitude of activities and services we offer. You’ll find Challenge Community Services in over 100 locations across NSW & QLD.

Our Purpose

We care about enriching every interaction, no matter how big or small, to make a positive difference in the lives of all members of our community. We do this because we are passionate about realising the potential of all people who we support.

Our Vision

We aim to be the industry leader in the empowerment and support of people within the community through integrated services and employment opportunities. We will positively impact and influence the development of social policy to enhance the communities we engage with.
